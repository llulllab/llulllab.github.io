Studio Llull

Studio Llull is the production unit of Llull Lab. It presents functional systems and engines developed from theoretical, mathematical, and methodological research. These productions are not commercial products in the conventional sense, but operational implementations of ongoing research.

Tropus

Generative music and theoretical system

A dual-mode system combining musical generation (Generata) and conceptual inquiry (Theoria). Developed as an applied extension of Tractatus de Musica and Sync-Turing methodology.

Status: Released
Website: tropus.io

Freud for Machines

A Computational Psychoanalytic Ontology

An ontological framework designed to model unconscious structures, repression, and symbolic mediation in machine systems. Developed for research in AI, representation, and machine psychoanalysis.

Status: Foundational framework
Website: Coming soon

Ambivalence-Centered Semantic Geometry Engine

Semantic and geometric modeling of ambivalence

A core engine designed to represent ambivalence as a structural and geometric property rather than a logical inconsistency. Serves as a semantic foundation for multiple Llull Lab systems.

Status: Core semantic engine
Website: Coming soon

Reflective Judgment Engine

Decision frameworks under normative uncertainty

An experimental framework designed to support reflective judgment processes in machine systems operating under normative uncertainty. Grounded in non-deterministic evaluation rather than rule-based ethics.

Status: Experimental
