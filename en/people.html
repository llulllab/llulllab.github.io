Collaborators & Partnerships

Llull Lab is an open platform for interdisciplinary experimentation, bringing together diverse expertise to address challenges no single field can solve alone.

We seek partners ready to challenge assumptions and co-invent new trajectories for transformation.

We collaborate through:

  • Joint research projects combining theoretical depth with practical application
  • Prototype development partnerships with artists, engineers, and technologists
  • Workshops and training for organizations navigating digital transformation
  • Strategic consultation for policymakers and technology developers

Current collaboration interests

  • Mathematical modeling for social systems
  • AI ethics and human–machine interfaces
  • Cultural analysis of technological change
  • Experimental approaches to organizational design
