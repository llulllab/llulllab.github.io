What Sets It Apart
  • 01Multi-perspective deliberation. Five independent expert perspectives (governance, legal, normative ethics, feasibility, and fundamental rights) evaluate each case separately and vote. Structurally reduces single-viewpoint bias.
  • 02Anti-totalitarianism safety module. Every deliberation is screened for totalitarian risk patterns before a decision is finalized. The system automatically halts if extreme risk patterns are detected. Designed to err on the side of caution.
  • 03Concrete decision framework. A preliminary analysis defines what approval, rejection, and modification specifically mean for each case. Evaluators vote on defined actions, not abstract opinions.
  • 04Precedent-grounded reasoning. Each case is evaluated in light of prior ethical cases and precedent decisions. Deliberation quality improves as the precedent library grows.
  • 05Built for autonomous systems. Deliberation output integrates directly into real-time decision pipelines: autonomous vehicles, robotic systems, and automated decision infrastructure. Designed to operate at machine speed, not committee speed.
  • 06Full audit trail. Case description, expert analyses, vote breakdown, safety check, and reasoned decision, all persisted and exportable. Built for regulatory review and compliance documentation.
