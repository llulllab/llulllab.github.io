What Sets It Apart
  • 01Ambivalence, not sentiment. Where sentiment tools classify text as positive/negative/neutral, ASGE detects the simultaneous presence of opposing semantic structures. A categorically different analytical layer.
  • 02Psychoanalytic theoretical foundation. Grounded in the Freudian concept of ambivalence — the simultaneous operation of opposing principles within a system. Over a century of clinical validation, now made computable.
  • 03Hybrid architecture. Rule-based lexical detection for speed and determinism, combined with deep contextual analysis for nuance. Most tools are one or the other. ASGE uses both in a single pipeline.
  • 04Temporal geometry. Ambivalence as a dynamic curve over time, not a static score. When opposing meanings intensify matters as much as what was said.
  • 05Semantic Geiger counter. Four-level graduated auditory alerts with escalating frequencies and shifting waveforms. Ambivalence density is perceptible without watching the screen — designed for clinical and field settings.
  • 06Live interim analysis. Deep contextual analysis provides ongoing analytical feedback during live recording — not only after the session ends. Continuous interpretation while the conversation is still in progress.
