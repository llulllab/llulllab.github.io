The Problem

A professional short film screenplay costs hundreds to thousands and takes days to weeks. Independent filmmakers, students, and small production teams cannot afford this for every idea they want to explore. Existing writing tools produce prose, not production-ready screenplays. There is no service that takes a story idea and delivers a professional, structured short film screenplay in under a minute.

How It Works
01
Submit
Describe your story in plain language: plot, characters, setting, tone. Any genre, four languages.
02
Generate
The system produces a professionally formatted short film screenplay, grounded in a library of thousands of professional screenplays. Each scene includes structured production metadata.
03
Verify
A second computational stage independently reviews the screenplay for structural integrity, pacing, and production readiness before delivery.
What Sets It Apart
  • 01Production-ready output. Not a story draft. A professionally structured screenplay with per-scene metadata: location, time, characters, mood, visual notes. Ready for storyboarding, shot planning, and casting.
  • 02Grounded in professional practice. Generation is informed by a library of thousands of professional screenplays, not generic text training data.
  • 03Two-stage quality control. Every screenplay passes through a second computational stage that independently verifies structure, pacing, and production readiness.
  • 04~1 minute delivery. From story idea to complete short film screenplay in approximately one minute.
