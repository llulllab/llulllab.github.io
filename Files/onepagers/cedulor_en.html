What Sets It Apart
  • 01Four programmes, one platform. No existing tool covers Horizon Europe, MSCA, ERC, and EIC with dedicated computational pipelines.
  • 02Conversation to Part B, end to end. The user talks, the system writes. Complete document, not a blank page, not a prompt guide.
  • 03Two-stage quality control. Your draft is reviewed by computational specialists trained on EU evaluation methodology before delivery. Eligibility checks, internal consistency, structural completeness.
