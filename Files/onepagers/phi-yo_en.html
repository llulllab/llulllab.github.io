How It Works

What Sets It Apart
  • 01Original theoretical foundation. Built on Freud for Machines: A Computational Psychoanalytic Ontology — an original academic framework. Interpretations draw on up-to-date theoretical content, not generic training data.
  • 02Cumulative personal intelligence. A personal symbol map tracks each symbol’s frequency, contexts, and evolving meaning. A dream journal stores full interpretations and metadata. Your profile accumulates observations across your entire history.
  • 03Expandable knowledge base. New theoretical frameworks can be incorporated over time. Interpretive depth is not frozen at launch — it grows.
  • 04Eight languages, natively generated. English, Turkish, French, German, Spanish, Japanese, Russian, Arabic. The system generates in the detected language — not translated from English.
  • 05Ethical guardrails. Probabilistic language, no diagnosis, professional referral when concerning patterns emerge. Positioned as wellbeing support and self-reflection, not therapy.
  • 06Integration-ready architecture. REST API enables third-party platforms to embed Phi-Yo as a conversational wellbeing engine — from self-reflection and dream analysis to daily check-ins and emotional pattern tracking. Built for wellness apps, elder care companions, employee wellbeing programmes, and therapy support tools.
