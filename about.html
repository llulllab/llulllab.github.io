Llull Lab – Social Sciences for Action

Llull Lab is a research initiative intervening in social and political transformation through mathematical experimentation.

It responds to the epistemic and political challenges brought about by the convergence of large language models (LLMs), high-performance computing (notably GPU architectures), and robotic systems. This transformation is not only technological—it reconfigures the very conditions under which knowledge, labor, coordination, and meaning are produced.
