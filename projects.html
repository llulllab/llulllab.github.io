[Vision] – Why We Exist

Yet, social sciences—both in theory and in method—have largely remained peripheral to this shift. Llull Lab seeks to address this by developing new conceptual tools and mathematical models that enable social theory to engage actively with these ongoing transformations.

Our goal is twofold:
– to enhance the capacity of social sciences to intervene in this historical transition,
– and to ensure that this transition itself can benefit from the critical, ethical, and imaginative contributions of the social sciences.

Llull Lab operates at the interface between knowledge production and technological development. We collaborate with academic researchers, policy makers, and technology developers to formulate frameworks of understanding and action that are attuned to the emerging realities of our time.
